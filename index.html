lafelix hotel

La Felix Hotel is a 3-star hotel located at Pham Ngu Lao street, District 1. It has 51 elegant rooms with most rooms have the nice view. All rooms are fully equipped with modern facilities, double-glazed windows and luxurious bathrooms.

From our hotel rooms, you can have an amazing view to the 23-9 park nearby; feeling the fully green nature and peaceful atmosphere every morning.

Our hotel is extremely close to Ben Thanh Market, just 5 minutes of walking so it’s very convenient for your travel purpose. It’s also surrounded by many travel companies, restaurants, banks, cars & motorbikes rental services as well as exciting attractions.
